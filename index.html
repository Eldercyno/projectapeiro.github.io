Mission

Project Apeiro aims to determine cosmic radiation flux in the lower stratospheric regions of the earth's atmosphere over the Indian subcontinent. This study will help to determine radiation exposure risks to aviation and space flight crews. The experiment will be performed by deploying a cosmic radiation detector at an altitude of 22 kms above sea level using High Altitude Ballooning (HAB).

What is
Cosmic Radiation?

High energetic charged particles, originating in outer space, that travel at nearly the speed of light and strike the Earth from all directions.

How do we reach
Near Space?

The experimental payload will be mounted on a High Altitude Balloon (HAB) which will be launched from TIFR National Balloon Facility, Hyderabad.
