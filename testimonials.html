Dr. Satyanarayana Bheesette
Scientific Officer (H) TIFR

"Let me say that more than the scientific merit of this project, it is the spirit of these students to engage in scientific instrument building that is of very high significance. In the days of motivation of typical engineering students to get into software and management, these bunch of bright undergraduate students chose to do experimental high energy physics.......There have been some concerns of effect of cosmic rays on the people and instruments who travel at high altitudes and space. It is therefore useful to measure and study these aspects."

Javnika More
Airline Cabin Crew

"I’ve realized that regular flying certainly has biological impacts on us, though they’re different for every person. Although we don’t realize them in our day-to-day lives, over an extended period, they certainly can be observed.......I feel that there should be more scientific studies in this field which can lead to better understanding of the problems which can lead us to finding the right solutions for the problems. I appreciate this student effort to study radiation exposure risks for commercial aviation and wish them luck!"
